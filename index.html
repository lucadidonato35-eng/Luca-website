The Photographer
A Lens Held to the World

Luca Di Donato has spent the better part of a decade moving through remote places with a camera, a notebook, and the patience that wild subjects demand. Born in Milan, drawn outward. His work lives at the intersection of wildlife documentary and portraiture — the moment before language, where instinct and light say everything.

From the fjords of Norway to the coral atolls of the Maldives, from Spain's sierras to the streets of Seoul — Luca shoots exclusively in natural light. The work is quiet. It asks to be looked at slowly.
