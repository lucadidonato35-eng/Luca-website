About the Photographer

A patient witness to wild places.

Luca Di Donato is an Italian photographer whose work moves between wildlife observation and human stories. Working in natural light and long field sessions, he builds images that balance intimacy, atmosphere, and narrative depth for gallery walls and global campaigns.
